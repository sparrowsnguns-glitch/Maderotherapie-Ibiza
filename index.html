What is Maderotherapy?

Maderotherapy is a gentle to intensive wood-rolling massage using specialized tools to improve circulation, reduce swelling, refine body contours, and enhance skin texture. On Ibiza, we combine it with sea-inspired rituals and relaxing essential oil blends for a luxurious, feminine wellness experience.
